SERVICE CENTER!

Our top-notch service staff verify the boats to be in very good conditions and to be sold without any problems.

MMZ Tractor Export offer Tractor+ Digger +Trailers, Machinery, from private sellers, and clients of our company.  We also have contracts with competent authorities to receive Machinery from the bank mortgage, the price for them is lower because only the remaining price has to be paid.

All Machinery and their documents are checked by our team and are ready to be registered on behalf of the new owner.

We check all the boats very well when we accept to offer them for sale. Machinery are checked at the bottom, if needed they are refurbished and sold in very good conditions.

We have a truck equipped that deliver the Machinery in maximum safety.

We offer delivery if you buy from us, and we can deliver the Machinery in conditions of maximum safety.

All machinery have valid documents.
